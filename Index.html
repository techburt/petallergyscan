Frequently Asked Questions

Is PetAllergyScan really free?

Yes! Core features including unlimited scanning and one pet profile are completely free forever. We offer optional premium features like multiple pet profiles and custom allergen lists for power users.

Does this replace my veterinarian?

Absolutely not. PetAllergyScan is an educational tool to help you make informed decisions. Always consult your veterinarian before making dietary changes for your pet. We provide information, not medical advice.

How accurate is the scanning?

We use advanced text recognition technology to detect ingredients. However, OCR isn't perfect - poor lighting, small text, or worn labels can affect accuracy. Always double-check the results yourself and consult your vet.

What allergens can it detect?

We include 20+ common pet food allergens including chicken, beef, dairy, wheat, corn, soy, fish, and more. You can also create custom allergen lists for your pet's specific needs.

When will it be available?

We're launching in 2025 for both iOS and Android. Join our beta list above to be notified when we launch!

Does it work for cats and dogs?

Yes! PetAllergyScan works for any pet that eats commercial pet food - dogs, cats, and other small animals.
